Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

The complete process from initial consultation to visa approval typically takes 4-6 months. However, this can vary based on your chosen destination, program start date, and individual circumstances. We recommend starting at least 8-12 months before your intended start date.

Our fees vary depending on the destination and level of support you need. We offer flexible packages from basic application assistance to comprehensive end-to-end support. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your specific requirements.

Most English-speaking countries require proof of English proficiency through IELTS, TOEFL, or PTE. However, some universities offer waivers if you've completed previous education in English. We can help determine your specific requirements and prepare you for these tests.

Yes! Most countries allow international students to work part-time during their studies (typically 20 hours per week during term time). Work rights vary by country - Canada, UK, Ireland, and Australia offer the most flexible work opportunities for students.

We maintain a 98% visa success rate across all destinations. Our experienced team ensures every application is thoroughly prepared with complete documentation and proper guidance for visa interviews, maximizing your chances of approval.

Absolutely! Our post-arrival support includes helping you find suitable accommodation, whether on-campus residence or off-campus housing. We provide guidance on neighborhoods, budgeting, and connecting with other students.

Typical requirements include: academic transcripts, English proficiency test scores, statement of purpose, letters of recommendation, CV/resume, passport copy, and financial documents. Specific requirements vary by country and institution - we'll provide you with a detailed checklist.

Many countries allow you to bring dependents (spouse and children) on a dependent visa. Requirements and work rights for dependents vary by country. Canada, UK, and Australia are particularly family-friendly for international students.
